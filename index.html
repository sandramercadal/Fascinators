NEIGHBOURHOODS / BARRIOS

Best neighbourhoods in Madrid

Justicia

Barrio de Justicia is one of Madrid’s grandest and most architecturally significant neighbourhoods.

Metro: Chueca
What it's like:

Paris in the heart of Madrid. A liveable and welcoming neighbourhood, right in the centre dating back to the 16th century. It has evolved into one of Madrid’s chicest and most sought-after locations. Chueca together with Salesas, the two Justicia neighbourhoods, are a magnet for contemporary art galleries, some of them among the best in Madrid.

Malasaña

Originally called 'Barrio de las Maravillas', its known for its alternative atmosphere, lively cafés and nightlife, trendy boutiques, flea markets and vintage shops.

Metro: Noviciado
What it's like:

A charming neighbourhood in the heart of Madrid that embodies the vibrant and bohemian spirit of the Spanish capital. The district exudes a unique blend of history and modernity. It has become a landmark for urban art, with colourful murals adorning the facades of buildings.
